Typology for News Classification

1. Endorsements
Articles whose primary focus is the public declaration of support for Zohran Mamdani by a politician, organization, labor union, or prominent individual.
Key Focus: The act of supporting or the identity of the supporter.
Exclusions: Campaign events (use Campaign) or policy alignment discussions (use Civil Policy).

2. Civil Policy (Policy Substance)
Articles that analyze or explain the substance and details of specific policies, legislative proposals, or ideological stances advocated by Zohran Mamdani.
Key Focus: The "what" and "how" of a policy (e.g., universal childcare plan details, specifics of a housing law).
Exclusions: The political strategy of debating or promoting a policy (use Campaign), or the post-election execution of policy (use Governance).

3. Controversy (Political Attack/Scandal) 
Articles that detail a scandal, negative accusation, or conflict aimed at Zohran Mamdani or his direct associations (family, key staff) with the intent to damage his character, reputation, or political standing.
Key Focus: Conflict, scandal, ethical questions, allegations of wrongdoing, or character attacks unrelated to specific policy mechanics.
Inclusions: Attacks by political opponents, investigations, accusations of inappropriate behavior, or fallout from controversial personal associations.

4. Campaign (Strategy & Electoral Process) 
Definition: Articles focused on the mechanics of the election process and the political strategy used to win the election.
Key Focus: Polling results, fundraising numbers, debate performance, get-out-the-vote efforts, campaign messaging strategy, and general election coverage.
Exclusions: Explicit endorsements (use Endorsements) or post-election transition activities (use Governance).

5. Social Cause 
Definition: Articles that discuss Zohran Mamdani in the context of broad social or cultural identity issues and historical patterns, rather than a specific political event or campaign strategy.
Key Focus: His identity as an immigrant, Muslim, or person of color; discussions of historical racism or social justice movements in a general sense; or the cultural significance of his rise.
Exclusions: Policy articles addressing social issues (use Civil Policy) or character attacks related to identity (use Controversy).

6. Governance (Administration & Transition)
Definition: Articles focused on the administrative activities of Zohran Mamdani after his election, covering the transition to office, appointments, and general administrative organization.
Key Focus: Announcing transition teams, cabinet and staff appointments, meetings with governmental bodies (when focused on administrative duties), and organizational changes within the city government.
Exclusions: Coverage of new legislative proposals or detailed policy substance (use Civil Policy).
